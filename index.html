The Xeno Ecosystem

The multi-layered system built around the core engine provides complete peace of mind regardless of your needs, and ensures complete nullification of potential threats.

  • SMART Data Management

    IoT data management tools ensure that all data is organized and easily accessible. Recognize patterns and evaluate system performance from anywhere in the world, and log off while the system analyses and encrypts the information for you.

  • Space-to-Pixel mapping

    The spatial awareness built into each unit ensures that your needs are accounted for, regardless of the kind of environment. No space too big or small, the deployment configurations are endless.

  • Fire Threat Detection

    Extensive testing and on-site learning ensures that all events are accounted for. Each event is assigned a threat level, from a transient spark to a growing open flame. User-specified responses for each threat level ensure that all situations are dealt with without escalation.

  • Directed Fire Fighting

    Truly automated fire fighting is now possible, thanks to the core engine the Nullify Xeno® is built around. Automated drone and robotic fighting systems are in development and provide immediate reponse to situations. Finally, a fire management system that will notify you of catastrophes that never happened.
